# Team

Who's on the project. Two sections with two different rules:

- **Owners** — must stay true. When ownership changes, the table changes the same day. A stale owners table is broken.
- **Contributors** — only grows. Names never come off, even after people move on. It's the project's memory of who built it.

## Owners

**What ownership means here:** the owner keeps their thread **moving and visible** — making sure it progresses, decisions get made, and the team always knows where it stands. It does **not** mean doing it alone. Everyone discusses and contributes to every area; the owner is the one accountable for the thread not stalling or going silent.

Individual **tasks** within a thread still get one clear owner each (see [docs/process.md](docs/process.md)).
