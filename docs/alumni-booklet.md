# Yearbook

> This document captures what data points matter for an alumni record —
> using the IIT Delhi Yearbook as a reference.
> It feeds directly into the data collection module of V1.

## Reference — IIT Delhi Yearbook (ColoredCow)

ColoredCow built a data collection tool for IIT Delhi ~8-9 years ago.
The purpose was to collect alumni data and publish it as a physical
coffee table booklet — one dedicated page per alumni — distributed
at the 25-year reunion of each passing batch.

Each alumni page in that booklet contained:

### Confirmed from the discussion
- **Photo** — a recent photo of the alumni
- **Name**
- **Batch year** — year of passing out
- **Branch** — which engineering department
- **Hostel** — which hostel they lived in during college
- **Current city**
- **Life after IIT Delhi** — what they have been doing since graduating
- **Favorite memories** — a short personal note
- **Life lessons** — something they would tell their younger self
- **Old photos** — scanned memories from college days
- **New photos** — recent life photos

### Inferred from context
- **Current role and company**
- **College stories** — personal memories from their time at college

### Added by the team
- **Family** — family details
- **Email**
- **Contact number**
- **College nickname** — what they were famously called in college or friend circle

## What This Means for KEC

The IIT Delhi reference gives us a proven starting point for what
data is worth collecting from KEC alumni. Not all fields will apply
directly — KEC's context is different — but this is the baseline
to work from.
