**Benefits:**

Alumni benefit by getting hiring opportunities and access to talented students from their own college.  
They gain recognition and personal branding through profiles, badges, and mentorship roles.  
The platform helps them network with other alumni for career growth, business, and opportunities.

For students there will be opportunities for jobs and internships that will be posted by Alumni’s

**Inside the page**

Inside the portal, users can access various features such as posting updates, liking and interacting with posts, connecting with other users, and real-time messaging.

Alumni can share job opportunities, internships, or any professional updates, while students and other users can post information about events happening in the college.

This platform creates an interactive environment where both alumni and students can engage, communicate, and stay updated with opportunities and activities.
