  
**Why do we Need an Alumni Portal**   
After completing their studies, many alumni gradually lose connection with their College. At the same time current students often struggle to find reliable guidance regarding college,internship, placement, career etc. college also face difficulty in maintaining records of these many passout students and tracking their achievements .

This portal will help to solve these problems by creating a dedicated platform where alumni, students and faculty members can engage and share knowledge 

**Its benefit**

  **To alumni**

They can have a direct connection with their own institute whose records and profile will be already available which will make recruitment and internship selection easier.  
To save time instead of having multiple Whatsapp group they can have a centralized platform and they can find skilled candidates quickly   
They can reconnect with their College after graduation and participate in its growth , get invited to alumni events and networking opportunities 

**Conclusion** \- hiring, time saving, networking, college connection 

   **To faculty** 

Faculty can collect direct feedback from alumni working in industry to understand current industry requirements and employers expectations.  
They can connect with alumni to create opportunities for collaborative projects, industrial training and practical learning experiences.  
Faculty can strengthen student career support through connections with alumni working in different industries   
This helps faculty build a strong and long \-lasting professional network around the department

   **To students** 

Students can receive career guidance and mentorship from experienced alumni working in different fields.  
They can learn about internship opportunities, placement processes and industry expectations.  
Students can build valuable professional connections at an early stage of their careers.  
They can gain practical knowledge and real world insights that are often not covered in academic courses.
