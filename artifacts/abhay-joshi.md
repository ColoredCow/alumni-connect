**ALUMNI CONNECTION PORTAL :-**

\***Problem**\-  
In our college,there is no centralized platform that effectively connects alumni with current students,due to which both sides faces problems like-

**Problems Faced by Students:**

* Lack of career guidance from experienced professionals of their respective fields.  
* Limited industry and professional connections.  
* Difficulty in finding internship,projects to work and placement opportunities.  
* Lack of educational guidance.  
   

**Problems Faced by Alumni:**

* Difficulty in staying connected with the college community after graduation.  
* No specific / easy to use platform for mentorship and guidance.  
* Lack of centralized platform to share about the internships,projects and job opportunities.  
* Reduced interaction between alumni and college over the time.

As there are students who need help and alumni who are willing to help.However,there is currently no simple,organized and efficient platform in the market to solve this problem.

**\*Purpose/Need \-**  
The Alumni Connect platform aims to establish a strong bridges between alumni and students by creating a centralized networking and mentorship ecosystem , the platform will \-

* Provide mentorship and  guidance related to both career and educational.  
* Helps students to get industrial insights and exposure.  
* Increase internship,placement and project opportunities.  
* Enable networking between alumni and student.  
* Increases alumni engagement with the college.  
* Create a community for knowledge and professional growth.


**\*Proposed Solution-**  
To solve the problem of communication gap between alumni and current students, we propose a centralized platform called **Alumni Connect.**

The platform will create a network where alumni and students can interact professionally,instead of students searching for guidance through personal contacts or social media, they can directly connect with alumni based on their career interests, company, or expertise.

**The platform will focus on three core functions:**  
**1\. Mentorship-**  
Students can request guidance from alumni regarding careers, higher studies, placements, and skill development.

**2\. Networking-**  
Students can build professional connections with alumni working in different industries and organizations.

**3\. Opportunity Sharing-**  
Alumni can share internships, job openings, projects, events, and industry-related opportunities directly with students.

By combining these three functions, the platform creates a structured and efficient ecosystem that benefits students, alumni, and the college.

**\*Working\-**

**Step 1: Registration**  
**(Alumni Registration)**

* Alumni sign up using college email, graduation year, branch, and professional details.  
* Admin verifies alumni accounts before activation.

**(Student Registration)**

* Students register using their college email and academic details.  
* Verified student accounts gain access to the platform.

**Step 2: Profile Creation**  
**(Alumni Profile)**

* Name  
* Graduation Year  
* Department  
* Current Company  
* Job Role  
* Areas of Expertise  
* LinkedIn Profile

**(Student Profile)**

* Name  
* Department  
* Year of Study  
* Skills  
* Career Interests

**Step 3: Search and Connect**  
**Students can search alumni based on:**

* Industry  
* Company  
* Department  
* Graduation Year  
* Skills

**(Students can send connection or mentorship requests to alumni.)**

**Step 4: Mentorship System**

* Alumni can accept or decline requests.  
* Student and alumni can schedule meetings.  
* Chat and video meeting options can be added.

**Step 5: Opportunities Section**  
**Alumni can post:**

* Internships  
* Job Openings  
* Freelance Opportunities  
* Events and Workshops

**(Students can apply directly through the platform.)**

**Step 6: Events and Networking**  
**The platform can host:**

* Alumni Meetups  
* Webinars  
* Career Guidance Sessions  
* Industry Talks  
*  Workshops

**(Students can register and attend these events.)**
